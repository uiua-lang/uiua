Introducing Uiua's new official mascot: Kala the cuttlefish!

Kala's pronouns are she/her. She will now appear in various places on the Uiua website. Feel free to use images of her anywhere!

Uiua now continues the rich history of programming languages having fun animal mascots.

Kala's design was inspired by artwork from Uiua community members Jacob Lockwood and Æroplane:

Kala's vector design was created by artist Nikola D.

The commission of this art was made possible by the generosity of Sonja Lang, creator of Toki Pona and a wonderful friend to the Uiua community. Thank you so much! ❤️
